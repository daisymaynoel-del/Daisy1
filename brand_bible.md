# EASTEND Salon — Brand Bible
**Version 1.0 — Living Document. Update as the brand evolves.**

---

## 1. Identity

### Brand Name & Core Offering
**EASTEND** is an East London hair and beauty salon offering colour, cutting, styling, and beauty services — and the education to maintain your look between visits.

### Origin Story
Built by someone who came up the hard way in the hair industry, EASTEND exists to create the opportunities that weren't there before — a space where stylists can grow, where clients feel genuinely supported, and where everyone is part of a team rather than a transaction.

### Mission
Reach the highest level of success possible by building such strong demand and visibility that slow days become the exception, not the norm.

### 5-Year Vision
Fully booked most days. A recognisable name in East London's creative community. A salon that is known not just for great hair but for the culture it builds around it.

### Brand Personality (Three Words)
**Authentic. Welcoming. Vibrant.**

### The Brand as a Person
Real. Not a character, not a persona — a genuine human presence that is warm, skilled, and proud of where it comes from.

2. **Welcomed** — this place is for them
3. **Educated** — they learned something useful

## 4. Legal & Compliance

## 5. Visual Identity

### Aesthetic Direction
**Colourful, eye-catching, and warm** — inspired by Bluetit Salon's energy. The environment should feel inviting and vibrant, not sterile or editorial.

Not minimalist. Not magazine. Not cold. Real people, real transformations, real warmth.

### Faces & On-Camera Presence
- Faces shown: yes — stylists and clients (with consent)
- Preferred shot types: transformation reveals, behind-the-scenes process, talking-head education, b-roll of hands and hair, lifestyle
- Always subtitle videos

### Visual Rules
- Always subtitle (captions on every video)
- Show the product of the work — before/after, process, result
- Avoid looking like a hair magazine or lookbook (no cold, editorial distance)
- Logo/branding visible but not dominant

### Brand Colours (Direction)
Warm, vibrant, East-London energy. Until exact hex codes are confirmed by the owner:
- **Primary:** Deep terracotta / coral
- **Accent:** Warm gold
- **Background:** Cream / off-white
- **Dark:** Near-black charcoal

*Owner to confirm exact hex codes and provide logo files.*

## 6. Voice & Tone

| Attribute | Standard |
|-----------|---------|
| POV | "We" (first-person plural) |
| Register | Casual, warm, approachable |
| Swearing | No |
| Humour | Wholesome — light, fun, never mean |
| Audience address | Direct, like talking to a friend |

### Topics We Talk About (Active)
1. Hair care education (how to maintain your look at home)
2. Client transformations
3. Behind-the-scenes salon process
4. Product recommendations and usage
5. Seasonal hair trends
6. Team and culture moments
7. East London lifestyle and community
8. Booking tips and availability
9. Hair health and sustainability
10. Styling tips and tutorials

### Topics That Are Off-Limits
1. Politics (all topics — hard avoid)
2. Competitor salons (never name or reference)
3. Anything divisive or controversial
4. Explicit content
5. Anything that conflicts with the welcoming, inclusive brand ethos

### Words/Phrases We Love
- "Your hair, your way"
- "Book in" / "Come through"
- "The team"
- "East London's own"
- Community, growth, real

### Words/Phrases We Avoid
- Corporate jargon
- Anything that sounds scripted or salesy
- "Luxury" as a distancing word (we're accessible luxury, not exclusionary)

## 10. Content Production Rules

### Every Post Must:
- [ ] Align with one of the 4 content pillars
- [ ] Include subtitles/captions if video
- [ ] Have an optimised hook in the first 3 seconds
- [ ] Include a CTA (book, follow, DM)
- [ ] Use a trending sound that fits the brand voice
- [ ] Include a relevant hashtag set (mix of niche + broad)
- [ ] For any colour content: include patch test notice

### Flag for Human Review if:
- Predicted to underperform by >50% vs. rolling average
- Content could conflict with brand bible
- Audio has explicit lyrics (borderline cases)
- Anything involving real client faces where consent isn't confirmed

### Red Lines (Never Cross)
- No political content
- No referencing competitor salons
- No fake engagement or bot activity
- No copyrighted audio outside the licensed trending library
- No content that makes EASTEND look like a magazine rather than a real salon
- No explicit content
- No content posted between 22:00–00:00
